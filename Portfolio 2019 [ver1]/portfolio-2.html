Checkpoint

Magazine & Website Design

The Narrative

Checkpoint is a fictional company aim to target students to raise esteem and boost confidence. The message of this project is show that all the focus on success makes us forget the learning experience and process.

The objective of the project to create a magazine and website that allows students to find tips and suggestions for mental health and encourage study habits.

The Progess

This is the website for Checkpoint. My view is to keep it in a grid for organization. As a troublesome student, too much information on a page could be overwhelming. The color scheme is mostly black as the younger generation often prefer a darker theme but a tint of brown to relects colour of deska dn tables and to symbolize the feeling of stability, peace, relaxation and warmth. The colour of wholesome. Bright images are used to contact this. I also wanted to combine different medias than just having pictures or text on the page. Different components of medias was inserted for unquiness.


The End Product

This is the maganize design of Checkpoint. The pawn is purposely used to symbolize the process and meaning of project. The pawn needs to take one step at a time to reach the other side of the board. There will be difficult obstacles (chess pieces) standing in its way. However with great motive. it too, can reach great success. The image is off-center to stand out and also to represents the number 1.
