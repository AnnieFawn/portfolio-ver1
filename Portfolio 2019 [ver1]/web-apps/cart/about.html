About Us

Cart was established in 2019 by five hungry students at BCIT: Jessica, Matt, Annie, Cara and Janet. Tired of slow elevator rides that took up most of their lunch break, someone asked - what if lunch came to us?

It seems we are not alone. Over the last two weeks this simple idea has grown into a business servicing 24 buildings in Vancouver's Downtown core and 4 buildings in Surrey Centre.

The secret to our success is summed up in three words: Fresh, Nutritious, Affordable.
