How It Works


Create your mobile account. Our registration process takes about 15 minutes. We want to get to know you - dietary preferences, allergies, location, and payment info.


Each day you can choose from one of five meal choices - each inspired by one of our founders.


As a weekly subscriber, you can recieve between 1-5 lunches a week*. Preorders are hand-delivered to your desk, or you can choose from our cart when we come by. All lunches are $9.50, so there is no need for cash or daily payments.
